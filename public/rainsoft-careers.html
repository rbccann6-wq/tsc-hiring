About the role

You'll be the person who keeps our service department running smoothly and our technicians moving. When customers call about their water softener, reverse osmosis system, or whole-house filtration, you're the one they talk to — you get the ticket built, the tech scheduled, the parts ordered, and the follow-up done. It's the kind of job where organized, detail-oriented people thrive.

What you'll do

  • Answer service calls with warmth and professionalism
  • Build service tickets and schedule service appointments
  • Dispatch technicians and manage the daily service board
  • Create detailed work orders with the right parts and history
  • Order parts and keep techs stocked on the truck
  • Process warranty claims and manufacturer paperwork
  • Follow up on every completed service job — collect payment, confirm the fix, handle callbacks
  • Keep the CRM spotless — service history, notes, open tickets
  • Handle service invoicing and follow up on open balances
This role is service only. You won't be handling sales leads, new installs, or water test scheduling — that's a separate team. You focus 100% on taking care of customers who already own a RainSoft system.

What we're looking for

  • Friendly, upbeat phone presence — you're the voice of the department
  • Organized and detail-oriented — nothing slips through the cracks
  • Comfortable dispatching and juggling a moving schedule
  • Strong computer skills, comfortable learning new software
  • Previous service writer, dispatcher, or service coordinator experience is a big plus
  • Bonus: experience in HVAC, plumbing, pest, water treatment, or appliance repair
  • Dependable, punctual, takes ownership

What we offer

  • Competitive hourly pay based on experience
  • Monday–Friday schedule — no nights, no weekends
  • Paid time off and paid holidays
  • Tight-knit team with real growth opportunity
  • In-office role in Enterprise, AL
